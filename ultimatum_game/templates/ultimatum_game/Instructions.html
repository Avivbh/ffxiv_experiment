Instructions

You will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant.
In this study, one of you will be the proposer and the other will be the responder. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role, which will be randomly assigned.
On each round the groups and roles will be shuffled.

There is {{ Constants.endowment }} to split. the proposer will propose how to distribute this amount between himself/herself and the responder.
The responder will decide whether to accept to decline the proposal.
If the responder accepts the gil will be distributed according to the proposal.
If the responder declines, None of the players will get any gil.
